Rules and Event Structure

*Bird's-eye view on the SoP of the quiz*
1. There will be a preliminary round consisting of 20-25 questions, where questions will be projected on the screen. Per se students will scribble down the answers on papers provided. After the whole cascade of questions volunteers will check their answers,which would again be exhibited on the screen.
2. Top 6 teams will make it into the finals. They will battle through different rounds which include:*Pounce and Bounce round, Audio- visual written rounds,Clue round,Long visual connect round,Theme round* etc.
3.In case of a tie it would be resolved by asking some questions.
