Event Overview:

Day 1
1. First Round (Solo Matches):
- Each individual plays 2 Games.
- Top 16 players advance to the next round.
2. Second Round (1v1 Matches):
-Head-to-head matches.
-From the 16 players, 8 advance to the next round based on their score and performance.
3. Third Round (1v1 Matches):
- From the 8 players, 4 top players are selected.
- Winners advance to the next level.
Day 2
4. Fourth Round (1v1 Matches):
- From the 4 players, 2 top winners are selected.
- Head-to-head matches to determine the finalists.
5. Fifth Round (1v1 Matches):
- Final 1v1 match.
- The top winner is selected.
